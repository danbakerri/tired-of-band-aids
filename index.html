Are you tired of band-aids?

Counseling for many is seen as a support by those interested in inviting change to their lives. By exploring the connection between emotions, thoughts, and behaviors; one can remove the inoperative band-aids and revive one’s inner harmony.

Erica Baker, LMHC

Erica Baker is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor in Massachusetts and Rhode Island. She has been working in the sectors domestic violence, addiction, higher education and community mental health for the past 14 years.

Her passion is to support others in resolving the issues which hold them back in their daily lives. When she is not counseling she enjoys biking, going to the beach and spending time with her family.
